Grammy's Overnight Lasagna

Ingredients

    1 1/2 pounds ground beef
    1 30 oz jar marinara sauce
    3/4 cup water
    12 lasagna noodles
    1 16 oz package cottage cheese
    12 oz grated mozzerella cheese
    1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

    Step 1: Cook the ground beef in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until browned and no longer pink, about 5 minutes; drain fat. Stir in the marinara sauce, water, and salt. Bring to a boil. Remove sauce from heat.
    Step 2: Arrange 6 noodles in a 9x13-inch baking dish, breaking if necessary to fit. Spoon 1/2 of the sauce over noodles. Top with 1/2 of the cottage cheese and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers with remaining noodles, sauce, cottage cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Top with Parmesan cheese. Cover tightly with foil; refrigerate overnight.
    Step 3: Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    Step 4: Bake lasagna, covered, until noodles are soft, cheese is melted, and sauce is bubbly, about 1 hour. Allow to sit for 10 minutes before serving.
